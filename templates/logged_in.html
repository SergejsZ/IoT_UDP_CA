Key Features Of Our App

Transport4U has many unique features which is why it's probably the best Transport App in the world. Transport4U offers Passenger Tracking, Bus GPS Location and a QR Ticket Scanner.

Passenger Tracking

Passenger Tracking allows for you to track how full the bus is before it reaches your stop.

Bus GPS Location

Precise Bus Locations allows you to track how far away your bus is with a rough estimate on its arrival.

QR Ticket Scanner

With purchased tickets you can scan your QR Code on the bus to speed up stop times to get to your destination faster.

F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I search for specific bus routes and times?

    Yes you can! You can do this by clicking search at the top of the home page or by selecting timetables in the navigation bar.

  • How can I track the buses location?

    You can track the buses location after purchasing a ticket. You can do this by clicking my tickets in the navigation bar from there scroll down to find chosen stop.

  • Is the bus GPS tracking accurate?

    The bus tracking is fairly accurate giving a margin for error of between 5-10minutes this is to take into consideration the buses speed and traffic on the road.

  • How do I use my ticket?

    You use your ticket upon entering the bus. You present the ticket to the bus driver and he/she will scan your ticket. You will hear a beeping sound once the ticket is scanned.

  • How do I track the bus capacity?

    You can track the bus capacity the same way as you track the buses location. By scrolling down on the my ticket page to nus capacity. There the capacity is given in %.
